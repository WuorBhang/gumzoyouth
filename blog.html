LGBTQ+ Rights and Activism in Kenya

The LGBTQ+ community in Kenya has faced significant challenges in terms of legal recognition and societal acceptance. Same-sex unions remain illegal in Kenya, but there have been efforts by activists to fight for the rights of the LGBTQ+ community. In a significant development, the Supreme Court of Kenya criticized the government for its failure to register an association for LGBTQ+ people, stating that it discriminates against the rights of the community. The court ruled that everyone has the right to association, even though same-sex unions are still illegal in the country. This ruling was seen as a victory for the LGBTQ+ community in Kenya

The Gay and Lesbian Coalition of Kenya (GALCK)

The Gay and Lesbian Coalition of Kenya (GALCK): GALCK is a prominent organization that advocates for LGBTQ+ rights in Kenya. Although they don't have a dedicated blog, their website provides news updates, statements, and resources related to LGBTQ+ issues in the country

News articles and reports

Various news articles and reports cover the ongoing struggles and victories of the LGBTQ+ community in Kenya. These sources provide insights into the legal battles, societal challenges, and activism surrounding LGBTQ+ rights in the country.
